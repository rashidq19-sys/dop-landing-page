Landing Page Structure — Two Approaches

Same content, different storytelling strategies. Which flow feels right for DSP owners?

A) Problem → Solution Flow

Lead with pain, then reveal how DSPOps solves each one

1. Hero — "Run your entire DSP from one screen" + email capture + app screenshot
2. Trust Bar — "Built by an OSM who lived the 4 AM chaos" + key stats
3. The Problems — Spreadsheet scheduling, separate apps, no visibility, manual pay
4. For Owners — Visibility, payment control, accountant data, performance tracking
5. For OSMs — Morning dispatch, live rota, attendance, van handover, no spreadsheets
6. Your Drivers Get Their Own Portal — One app, shifts, pay, damage — they stop chasing you
7. How It Works (Quick) — 3 steps: Sign up → Connect data → Save hours
8. A Morning With DSPOps — Storyboard: 5:30 AM dispatch walkthrough
9. Before vs After — Side-by-side: chaos vs calm
10. Cost Calculator — "How much is your current setup costing you?"
11. Video Demo — 30-second product walkthrough
12. Pricing — 3 tiers (same as current)
13. FAQ — Common questions
14. Final CTA — Get early access + Book a demo

Strengths

  • Classic SaaS structure — proven to convert
  • Problems section creates urgency before showing solutions
  • Audience sections feel personal ("this is about ME")

Tradeoffs

  • More sections = longer page
  • Problems section might feel negative before they see the solution

B) Audience-First Flow

Skip the problem list — jump straight to "here's what it does for YOU"

1. Hero — "Run your entire DSP from one screen" + email capture + app screenshot
2. Trust Bar — "Built by an OSM who lived the 4 AM chaos" + key stats
3. For Owners — Visibility, control, accountant data, performance (with screenshots)
4. For OSMs — Morning dispatch, live rota, attendance, van handover (with screenshots)
5. Your Drivers Get Their Own Portal — Shifts, pay, damage — they stop bothering you
6. A Morning With DSPOps — Storyboard: 5:30 AM dispatch walkthrough
7. Before vs After — Side-by-side comparison
8. Cost Calculator — "How much is your current setup costing you?"
9. Video Demo — 30-second product walkthrough
10. How It Works — 3 steps: Sign up → Connect → Save hours
11. Pricing — 3 tiers
12. FAQ
13. Final CTA — Get early access + Book a demo

Strengths

  • Shorter — gets to value faster
  • No negativity upfront — leads with solutions
  • Each audience sees themselves immediately

Tradeoffs

  • Skips the "pain" setup — some owners might need that "yes, that's me!" moment first
  • Morning walkthrough carries more weight if problems were set up first
